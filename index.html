Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring students’ voices into the conversation over Arlington’s transportation future, advocating for a future in which streets are never hostile to students using any mode of transportation.

What We Offer

Design

Our goal is to design a way for people in the Arlington community to express their views and concerns on the safety of the roads for pedestrians.

Branding

We are a club dedicated to ensuring the safety of the people in our community hoping to spread new ideas to improve the way we transport around our community.

Consultation

We hope to get in touch with other orginizations that can help us have an even bigger impact on our community.

Promises

We promise to work as hard as we can to increase safety as much as we can.
